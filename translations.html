Why are quality translators important? Case study.

Apollo 11 Moon Landing (1969):

During the Apollo 11 mission, interpreters played a crucial role in facilitating communication between astronauts and ground control. The famous words "That's one small step for [a] man, one giant leap for mankind" were transmitted with the help of interpreters.

Fall of the Berlin Wall (1989):

Interpreters were instrumental in facilitating communication during the protests and events leading to the fall of the Berlin Wall. They helped convey the sentiments of the people and the changing political landscape. Quote: "As the Berlin Wall crumbled, interpreters stood on the front lines of history, ensuring that the voices of change and unity were heard loud and clear in multiple languages."
